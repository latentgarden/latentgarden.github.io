Trustworthy Machine Learning

Assignment 3, 3.2.1 Proof image

A machine trained with care, 
Inductive bias in its hair, 
To classify data with a glance, 
And improve with each circumstance.

But what happens when its test, 
Is something it hasn't guessed, 
An out of distribution shift, 
That makes its answers adrift.

A trustworthy model must know, 
When it's on uncertain ground, 
And the confidence in its flow, 
Should decrease when it's confounded.

So let's not trust machines so fast,
 Until we're sure they've been designed, 
To handle shifts from the past,
 And keep their answers well aligned.

Every end is a new beginning
